What is Civitas Dei?

Having trouble finding church announcements on Facebook or Whatsapp?

Have you ever had to search through a million messages to find the announcements your Ministry has made?

Say goodbye to those days!

Civitas Dei is a solution designed as a one way communication platform. Users who create an account can follow a Church or Ministry, where the latest announcements are displayed in the feed. This way, you can be kept up to date with the latest upcoming events!
